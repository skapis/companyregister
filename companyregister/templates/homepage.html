Get data about companies, entrepreneurs directly from business registers

On this site you can request business registers to get data about companies and entrepreneurs. Application is easy-to-use. To use this web application you have to create an account and then activate it. After that you can request data about your companies and entrepreneurs. Your data are not stored in any database. After you send requests application get data directly from business register and show to you in user-friendly form. After you refresh site the data are gone. You have only 100 requests per day, because it is a free version of application and we do not have resources to provide more requests.
